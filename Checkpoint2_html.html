Admissions

To gain entry to Pharo School Homosha, girls must score at least 50% mean score in their
Primary regional exam. A student must also pass the school's Grade 9 entrance exam and
score at least 50%. The tuition, meals, accommodation fees are 100% subsidised with
limited health care but parents have to provide all the basic essentials needed for boarding
life.

Supporting documents

  • Pass entrance assessments.
  • Fill out the admission and medical forms.
  • Submit a copy of regional exams certificate and IDs for parents or guardian.
  • By signing the admission documents, parents/guardians imply that they have read, understood and accepted all school rules.
