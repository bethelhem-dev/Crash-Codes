Pharo School Homosha is a leading boarding school for girls, offering secondary education for grades 9-12 in Benishangul Gumuz Region, Ethiopia. We follow the Ethiopian National Curriculum with aspects of international best practice. The school provides a fully-funded 4-year secondary education and boarding experience to female students. Our small class sizes, well-resourced facilities, dedicated team of educators, and small volunteer base accelerate student learning and the development of English and life skills. They also boost career prospects while building confidence, creativity, and critical thinking skills.



Pharo School Homosha was established in 2018 and officially inaugurated on February 29, 2020 on 16 hectares of land as a state-of-the-art full board girls’ school, with a capacity of 240. The school aims to offer the best educational and boarding opportunities to bright and driven girls from across the region. Our focus on female education is in recognition of the challenges that girls encounter in accessing education, which prevent many from unlocking their full potential.

Pharo School Homosha transforms the lives of its students. Our innovative enrolment is intentionally selective to provide high-quality, empowering, and meaningful education for secondary school-aged young female students in the Benishangul Gumuz region, Ethiopia. See more on our admissions page.

Our educational philosophy is articulated around five principles:

1. Leadership – everyone is a leader.

2. Potential – everyone is a genius.

3. Change – change starts with me.

4. Education – develop the whole person.

5. Motivation – educators empower students to lead their learning.
